🏈 Game Day Guide — Vaught-Hemingway

🌈 The Grove

Gates open 4 hrs before kickoff. No tents for noon games. No glass containers. No RVs in The Grove proper. Ole Miss vs. SEC opponents = expect capacity crowds.

🚘 Parking & Traffic

Best options: Oxford Conference Center lots, Pavilion Park & Ride. University Ave & Jackson Ave congested 2 hrs before/after. Ole Miss Shuttle from Pavilion recommended for home SEC games.

🏛 Oxford Square Pre-Game

Proud Larry's, Double Decker stage, Ajax Diner, Boure, City Grocery, The Velvet Ditch, Lenora's. Square fills 3+ hrs before home kickoffs. Reservations critical for LSU, Georgia, Egg Bowl.

⏪ 2026 Home Kickoffs

Charlotte Sep 12 6:45 PM • LSU Sep 19 6:30 PM • Missouri Oct 17 2:30 PM • Auburn Oct 31 11 AM • Georgia Nov 7 Flex • Wofford Nov 21 11 AM • Egg Bowl Nov 27 11 AM

🏦 Vaught-Hemingway Stadium

Capacity: 64,038. Field: Astroturf. Located on Ole Miss campus, University Ave, Oxford MS 38677. Renovated south end zone 2023.

⛰ Swayze Field (Baseball)

Capacity: 11,477. Located adjacent to Vaught-Hemingway. Home of the Diamond Rebels. 2027 season opens February.
