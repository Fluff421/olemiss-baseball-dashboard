🌈 The Grove

America's most iconic tailgate. Gates open 4 hrs before kickoff. No tents for noon games. Ole Miss rules: no glass, no RVs in The Grove proper.

🚘 Parking & Traffic

Park-and-walk from Oxford Conference Center or Pavilion lots. University Ave & Jackson Ave congested 2 hrs before/after. Ole Miss Shuttle from Pavilion recommended.

🏛 Oxford Square

Rowan Oak, Double Decker stage, Proud Larry's, Ajax Diner, Boure, City Grocery, The Velvet Ditch. Square bars fill 3 hrs before kickoff on home Saturdays.
